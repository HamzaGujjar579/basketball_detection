What is Anomaly Detection?

Anomaly detection involves identifying patterns or events in data that do not conform to expected behavior.

In video surveillance, anomaly detection refers to the use of advanced algorithms and AI to automatically detect unusual activities or behaviors that may indicate security threats, operational issues, or safety hazards.

By using machine learning and advanced algorithms, anomaly detection systems can analyze vast amounts of video data in real-time, identifying anomalies that could go unnoticed by human observers.

Why Use Anomaly Detection?

Anomaly detection is used to detect unusual dangerous situations in public spaces or suspicious behavior by people and to stop dangerous situations from escalating further.

Smarter Security

Improved Security - Automatically detects suspicious activities, ensuring quick response to potential threats.

Lowering Costs

Reduces the need for constant human monitoring, freeing up resources and lowering operational costs.
